--- ABOUT SIST ---
SIST (School of International Studies & Technology) is a British higher education institution operating in Morocco with campuses in Casablanca, Rabat, and Tangier. SIST was established in 2001 in Casablanca, expanded to Rabat in 2011, and to Tangier in 2013. SIST is an associate college of Cardiff Metropolitan University (Cardiff Met), delivering the same degree programmes as those offered on Cardiff Met's UK campus. SIST is the first established British institute running Cardiff Metropolitan University programmes in Morocco.

SIST is unique in Morocco as the only private institute that is a member of the Association of Arab Universities. The institution is under the quality control of the Ministry of Higher Education in Morocco, Cardiff Metropolitan University, and the Quality Assurance Agency (QAA) for Higher Education UK.

SIST has delivered British Education in Morocco for over 25 years, awarding Undergraduate and Postgraduate degrees. The aim of SIST is to be positioned as the top business school in Morocco.

--- PARTNERSHIP WITH CARDIFF METROPOLITAN UNIVERSITY ---
SIST is an Associate College of Cardiff Metropolitan University. Students at SIST receive the same degree as if they had studied at Cardiff Met in the UK. Cardiff Metropolitan University guarantees that the education provided by their partners, including SIST, meets the same high standards as their UK campus. SIST students are eligible for a full scholarship covering the 3rd year of study in the UK, and more than 60 students have already benefited from this scholarship.

The British Council, Cardiff Metropolitan University, and SIST maintain a strong partnership. SIST also has partnerships with the British Council Morocco, NCG Group, and Cambridge Assessment English.

--- PROGRAMMES OVERVIEW ---
SIST offers three levels of programmes:

1. FOUNDATION YEAR
The Foundation Year is a pre-university level programme for students holding a baccalaureate. It is a full-time programme designed to enhance students' academic English level and improve study skills essential for undergraduate study. The programme equips students with the ability to use English in the classroom and to conduct academic research and deliver exceptional presentations. It is designed for non-English speakers who want to pursue British higher education. Duration: 4 to 8 months.

2. BAC+3 PROGRAMMES (BA / BSc) – Undergraduate
SIST Bac+3 Degrees provide an exceptional learning experience designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset required to succeed in today's competitive environment. Strong emphasis is placed on developing digital competencies, entrepreneurial thinking, and ethical values. Programmes available include:
- BA (Hons) Business Management (with pathways in Marketing, Finance, and other specialisations)
- BSc Software Engineering
- BSc Computer Science
Duration: 3 years (after Foundation Year or Baccalaureate with sufficient English level)
Language: 100% taught in English

3. BAC+5 PROGRAMMES (MA / MBA / MSc) – Postgraduate
SIST Bac+5 Degrees are offered across all three academic campuses. These advanced studies are designed to strengthen expertise, sharpen critical thinking, and prepare graduates for leadership roles in an increasingly globalised world.
- MBA (Master of Business Administration) – internationally recognised qualification for managers
- MSc programmes
Duration: 2 years after Bac+3
Language: 100% taught in English

--- DOUBLE DEGREE ---
Students at SIST receive two degrees upon successfully completing their programme:
1. International British Degree issued by Cardiff Metropolitan University (recognised globally)
2. SIST Degree accredited by the Moroccan Ministry of Higher Education (Private Moroccan BAC+3/BAC+5)

--- LANGUAGE OF INSTRUCTION ---
All programmes at SIST are taught fully in English. For students who are not yet proficient in English, SIST offers the Foundation Year programme to improve their academic English before starting their undergraduate degree.

--- ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS ---

Foundation Year:
- Moroccan Baccalaureate degree, high school diploma, A2 levels, or equivalent.

Bachelors (BA, BSc, BEng):
- Moroccan Baccalaureate degree, high school diploma, A2 levels, or equivalent.
- All applicants must pass the Oxford Placement Test with a score equivalent to IELTS 6.0, or provide an equivalent IELTS score.

Masters (MSc, MBA, MEng):
- A bachelor's degree from a recognised university with a GPA of 2.7/4.0 or GPA 3.5/5.0, or any other equivalent of a UK 2:2 classification.
- All applicants must pass the Oxford Placement Test with a score equivalent to IELTS 6.5, or provide an equivalent IELTS score.

--- SCHOLARSHIPS ---
SIST offers scholarships to high-achieving students:
- Students with high baccalaureate grades can apply for scholarships.
- SIST students are eligible for a full scholarship covering their 3rd year of study at Cardiff Metropolitan University in the UK.
- More than 60 students have already benefited from this UK scholarship.
- SIST participates in the ERASMUS+ scholarship programme.
- The finance department assists students in developing personalised financial plans.

--- TRANSFER TO THE UK ---
SIST students have the option to transfer to Cardiff Metropolitan University in the UK at any time during their studies. After successfully completing the first year at SIST, students can transfer to Cardiff Metropolitan University in the UK to continue their studies. Transferring students benefit from a 20% tuition fee discount at Cardiff Met.

Most students choose to study the full Bachelor programme in Morocco and pursue a specialised Master's degree at Cardiff Metropolitan University in the UK. Students can also complete their full university studies in Morocco and obtain both Bachelor and Master's Degrees from Cardiff Metropolitan University without leaving Morocco.

--- CREDIT TRANSFER ---
Students can transfer credits from other universities to SIST. The process involves submitting transcripts from other universities for review by Cardiff Metropolitan University.

--- EMPLOYABILITY ---
SIST places strong emphasis on graduate employability:
- 95% of SIST graduates are hired or in further study within 6 months of graduation.
- 94.8% of Cardiff Met graduates are working or continuing their studies within 6 months of graduation.
- 100% of students undergo a Placement Internship in their 2nd year (Level 5).
- Students study Work Placement with Personal Development in 2nd year to ensure competitive CVs and LinkedIn profiles.
- Some students receive job offers after their 2nd year internship and study and work simultaneously in 3rd year.
- Multinationals in Morocco reach out to SIST with English-speaking job offers.
- SIST holds a yearly Job Fair inviting multinational businesses such as OPPO, ARAMEX, and DELL.
- The majority of SIST students are trilingual: Arabic, French, and English.
